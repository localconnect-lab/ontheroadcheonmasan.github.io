About Local Connect – On The Road (Cheonmasan)

On The Road is a small local studio created by Local Connect, designed for travelers and locals who want to feel Busan slowly – through food, craft, and quiet time on the hill.

The space
  • Located on Cheonmasan hill in Seo-gu, Busan
  • Cozy indoor space suitable for small groups and private classes
  • Surrounded by local residential area, away from busy tourist streets
  • Perfect for slow travelers, friends, couples, and family groups
Who it’s for
  • International visitors who want to learn real Korean food, not just take photos
  • Travelers looking for meaningful, hands-on experiences in Busan
  • Locals who want to enjoy cooking, coffee, flowers, and craft in a calm space

Korean Cooking & Creative Classes

Choose from two signature Korean cooking classes and three creative healing workshops. All classes are small-group, hands-on, and suitable for beginners.

Main · Korean cooking

Gukbap & Kkakdugi Class

Learn how to make two of Korea’s most loved comfort foods: warm and hearty Gukbap (Korean pork soup with rice) and crunchy Kkakdugi (cubed radish kimchi).

This class focuses on traditional Korean home-style flavors and basic kimchi making. Perfect for travelers who want to taste and understand real local food.

· Duration: about 90–120 minutes
· Level: Beginner-friendly (no cooking experience needed)
· Includes ingredients, recipe card & tasting time
Main · Korean street food

Gimbap & Tteokbokki Class

Make Korea’s most famous street food combo: colorful Gimbap (Korean rice rolls) and spicy-sweet Tteokbokki (rice cakes in gochujang sauce).

A fun, interactive class where you roll, stir, taste, and take photos. Great for friends, couples, and family travelers.

· Duration: about 90 minutes
· Level: Beginner-friendly
· Includes ingredients, recipe card & photo time
Sub · Craft

Korean-style Craft Workshop

Enjoy a slow craft session making small handmade items using simple materials. A relaxing way to unwind after sightseeing.

· Focus on relaxation & creativity
· Beginner-friendly, no prior skills needed
· Suitable for groups and team-building
Sub · Flower

Flower Arrangement Class

Create your own flower basket or bouquet using seasonal flowers. A calm, emotional healing experience loved by many visitors.

· Seasonal designs tailored to your visit
· Gift-ready arrangements you can take home
· Great for couples, friends, and special days
Sub · Coffee

Coffee Brewing & Aroma Class

Experience Korean coffee culture through hand-drip brewing and aroma tasting. Learn how to make and enjoy a cup slowly, with simple mindfulness.

· Hand-drip brewing using local beans
· Aroma-based relaxation & gentle sharing time
· Works well as a short healing program

The On The Road Experience

We keep our classes small, warm, and practical. You don’t have to be good at cooking or craft – just come as you are, and enjoy the time.

  • ✓ Small-group & private – classes are kept small so you can relax, ask questions, and move at your own pace.
  • ✓ Local & personal – hosted by a Busan local who combines professional knowledge with a friendly, home-like mood.
  • ✓ Beginner-friendly – all steps are guided slowly with clear explanations; perfect even if it’s your first time.
  • ✓ Flexible for groups – programs can be adapted for families, friends, schools, and corporate teams.

If you are a travel agency, school, or company, we can provide a program outline and schedule proposal in both Korean and English.
